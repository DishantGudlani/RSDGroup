Mohammed

CEO & Managing Director

Mohammed's Journey: From Struggle to Leadership at RSD Group

Mohammed, the CEO & Managing Director of RSD Group, is a paragon of resilience, adaptability, and unyielding spirit. His life story is one of profound challenges and remarkable triumphs. Mohammed, as an only child, faced the world alone following the early demise of his guardians. Without siblings or friends to lean on, he shouldered immense responsibilities at a young age.
In the years following high school, Mohammed's life was a relentless battle for survival. He embraced every job imaginable – from being a driver, mechanic, labor, farmer, plumber, carpenter and cleaner and etc, to working as a taxi driver, Uber driver , Ola driver, railway ticket agent to travels and tourism administrator. These roles were not mere employment; they were lifelines in a sea of adversity, each one honing his skills in adaptability and resourcefulness.
This tumultuous period was further complicated by a desperate attempt to save his parents, leading to significant medical debt. The financial strain forced him to drop out of school, seemingly dashing his dreams and defying his parents' last wish for him to be the first in his family to graduate college. A local law back in the days that barred dropouts from re-entering university after three years seemed to seal his fate back in his country.
However, a twist of fate presented itself in the form of a scholarship opportunity in Hungary. This was not merely a chance for academic pursuit; it was a beacon of hope and a pathway to fulfill his and his parents' dreams. In Hungary, Mohammed embarked on a transformative journey. He pursued his studies diligently, simultaneously gaining practical experience in various corporate sectors, including regulatory reporting, business analyst, auditing,collection analyst, Financial analyst, and investment trader. These experiences reinforced his belief in the importance of understanding an employee's perspective to be a successful employer.
Mohammed's current expertise in finance, with specializations in accounting, investment banking, regulatory reporting, and data analytics, is a testament to his journey of growth and learning. His tenure in Hungary was more than an academic achievement; it was a period of honoring his parents' memory and realizing their aspirations for him.
Today, Mohammed leads RSD Group not just as its administrator but as its visionary. He brings to the company a unique blend of life experiences, a deep understanding of both struggle and triumph, and a commitment to social responsibility. His leadership is marked by empathy, a quality shaped by his own life's challenges. At RSD Group, Mohammed sees an opportunity to uplift others, echoing his path from hardship to success. His ambition to build legacies, especially in Hungary – the country that gave him so much – is at the heart of his mission. Mohammed envisions a world where every individual is treated with dignity and respect, a principle he embeds into the fabric of RSD Group.

Shekh

Co-Founder and Managing Director

Shekh's Journey: Breaking Stereotypes and Overcoming Adversity

Shekh, another Co-Founder and Managing Director of RSD Group, hails from a small city in India. His path was not typical; he faced significant obstacles right from the outset. Unlike many of his peers, Shekh did not aspire to become an engineer or a doctor. Instead, he chose to study Geology, a decision that was met with skepticism and ridicule from those around him, including his own family and friends. Despite the constant bullying and being labeled as someone bound to fail, Shekh remained steadfast in his pursuit.
His determination led him to a government university, a journey that required traveling 400 kilometers from his hometown. The challenges didn't end with his education. After graduating, Shekh struggled to find placement in his field. However, an opportunity arose in Hungary, where he was awarded a scholarship for his master's studies. This move proved to be transformative. In Hungary, Shekh not only pursued his academic interests but also explored his passion for the entertainment industry, taking on roles as an extra in movies and series.
During this time, Shekh encountered a stark disparity in treatment between local Hungarian extras and those from ethnic minorities. He noticed that, despite his ethnic background, he was often paid more than his Hungarian counterparts for the same work. This inequity, along with the poor quality of food and treatment meted out to extras, deeply troubled him. He felt compelled to address these injustices but found himself constrained by his status as a foreign student.
The turning point came when Shekh met Mohammed, the current CEO of RSD Group, at a film shoot. They shared a mutual dislike for the unfair practices they observed in the industry. Their meeting was fortuitous, as it led to a conversation by the Danube River, where they later met the IT Co-Founder. The trio, who started as strangers, quickly bonded over their shared experiences and struggles. They envisioned a company that would not only address the injustices they witnessed but also provide equal opportunities and respect to everyone, regardless of their background.
Thus, Shekh's role in RSD Group is pivotal. He brings his unique perspective, fueled by his experiences of being underestimated and his passion for challenging the status quo. His vision for the company includes creating a casting agency that ensures fair treatment and pay for all, irrespective of ethnicity, and providing quality food options like halal and kosher, catering to diverse dietary needs. Shekh's journey is a testament to resilience and the power of turning adversity into a force for positive change.

Dishant

Co-Founder

Dishant's Path: Triumphing Over Adversity

Dishant, a Co-Founder of RSD Group, is a testament to the power of perseverance and innate talent. Originating from a modest town in India, his journey was laden with formidable challenges. Unlike many in his surroundings, Dishant didn't have the luxury of abundant resources. His access to computers and the internet was minimal, yet his passion for technology and programming knew no bounds. In a country teeming with competitors, he stood out as a self-made prodigy in the realm of coding and software development.
Despite these constraints, Dishant's brilliance in programming and his unwavering commitment to his craft eventually landed him a coveted position in one of India's premier software companies. His exceptional skills and dedication led to a significant career milestone – a transfer to Hungary. This relocation was not just a professional upgrade; it was a doorway to a world that offered him a glimpse of freedom and a vibrant multicultural environment.
However, life in Hungary was not without its challenges. Dishant, a genius in his field, often found himself grappling with social hurdles. His introverted nature and unique outlook on life made it difficult for him to connect with others, leading to feelings of isolation and misunderstanding. The cultural shift was significant, and he frequently found himself on the receiving end of stereotypes, labeled a 'nerd' or 'weirdo', which only added to his sense of alienation.
The turning point in Dishant’s life came at the shores of the Danube River. It was here that he met Mohammed and another future Co-Founder of RSD Group. This meeting was more than just a chance encounter; it was the convergence of shared experiences and mutual understanding. They spoke of their trials and tribulations, and in this exchange, they found strength and camaraderie. This newfound bond laid the foundation for what would become RSD Group.
In RSD Group, Dishant plays a crucial role. He brings to the table not only his technical expertise and mastery over various programming languages and AI but also his personal experiences of overcoming social barriers and prejudices. His vision for the company is to create an environment where diversity is celebrated, and every voice is heard and valued. His journey is a narrative of transformation – from a lone genius battling societal norms in India to a key figure in a company that stands for inclusion, understanding, and innovation.
However, a twist of fate presented itself in the form of a scholarship opportunity in Hungary. This was not merely a chance for academic pursuit; it was a beacon of hope and a pathway to fulfill his and his parents' dreams.
